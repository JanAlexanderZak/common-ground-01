# SPD

The Sozialdemokratische Partei Deutschlands (SPD) is Germany's social-democratic party and one of its two traditional Volksparteien. As of 2024–25, the SPD has predominantly supported a reform of the debt brake (Schuldenbremse), in particular the introduction of an investment exemption to enable higher levels of public investment in infrastructure, climate, and defence.
