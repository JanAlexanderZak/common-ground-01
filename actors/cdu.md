# CDU/CSU

The CDU (Christian Democratic Union) and its Bavarian sister party CSU form a joint parliamentary group in the Bundestag and are Germany's main centre-right, christian-democratic political force. As of 2024–25, the CDU/CSU has predominantly supported retaining the debt brake (Schuldenbremse) in its current constitutional form, though internal voices have at times signalled openness to limited adjustments.
