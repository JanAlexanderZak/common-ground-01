# ifo Institut für Wirtschaftsforschung

The ifo Institut, based in Munich and affiliated with the Leibniz Association and Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München, is one of Germany's most prominent economic research institutes and the publisher of the monthly ifo Business Climate Index. In the debate on the German debt brake, leading ifo researchers have generally argued for retaining the rule and have warned against weakening its quantitative limits.
