# Die Linke

Die Linke is Germany's left-wing party, emerging from the merger of the PDS and WASG, with policy priorities centred on redistribution, expanded public services, and peace policy. As of 2024–25, Die Linke has predominantly supported abolishing the debt brake (Schuldenbremse) in its current form or replacing it with a fundamentally different fiscal framework that does not constrain public investment.
