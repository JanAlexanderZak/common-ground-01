# Deutscher Bundestag

The Bundestag is the directly elected federal parliament of Germany, exercising legislative power, electing the Federal Chancellor, and overseeing the federal government. It is the institutional venue in which the federal budget and any constitutional changes to the debt brake (Schuldenbremse) are debated and voted on.
