# Deutsche Bundesbank

The Deutsche Bundesbank is the central bank of the Federal Republic of Germany and an integral part of the Eurosystem. It publishes monthly and annual reports on monetary, financial, and fiscal developments and is a frequently cited authority on questions of public debt sustainability and the design of fiscal rules.
