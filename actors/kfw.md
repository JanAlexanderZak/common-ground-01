# Kreditanstalt für Wiederaufbau (KfW)

KfW is the federal promotional bank of Germany, owned by the federal government and the Länder, providing financing for housing, infrastructure, SMEs, climate, and municipal investment. KfW Research publishes the annual KfW-Kommunalpanel, a representative survey of German municipalities that is a primary source for estimates of the municipal investment backlog (Investitionsstau).
