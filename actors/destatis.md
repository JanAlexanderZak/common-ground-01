# Statistisches Bundesamt (Destatis)

Destatis is the Federal Statistical Office of Germany, responsible for collecting, processing, and publishing official statistics on the German economy, society, and public finances. It is a primary source for data on GDP, government debt, public investment, and the indicators used in fiscal-policy debates.
