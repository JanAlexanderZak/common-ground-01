# Bündnis 90/Die Grünen

Bündnis 90/Die Grünen is Germany's green party, with policy priorities centred on climate, ecological transformation, and civil rights. As of 2024–25, the Greens have predominantly supported a reform of the debt brake (Schuldenbremse), advocating for an investment exemption to finance climate-related and infrastructure investment.
