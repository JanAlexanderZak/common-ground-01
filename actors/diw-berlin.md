# Deutsches Institut für Wirtschaftsforschung (DIW Berlin)

DIW Berlin is an independent, non-profit economic research institute and a member of the Leibniz Association. In the debate on the German debt brake, DIW researchers have generally taken a reform-oriented stance, advocating for an investment exemption or a recalibration of the rule to accommodate higher public investment needs.
