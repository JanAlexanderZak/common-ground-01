# FDP

The Freie Demokratische Partei (FDP) is Germany's classical-liberal party, with policy priorities centred on market-liberal economic policy, fiscal discipline, and individual freedoms. As of 2024–25, the FDP has predominantly supported a strict application of the debt brake (Schuldenbremse) and has been the most vocal opponent in the federal coalition spectrum of weakening or substantially reforming the rule.
