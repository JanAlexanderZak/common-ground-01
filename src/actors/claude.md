# Claude (Anthropic)

Claude is a family of frontier general-purpose AI assistants developed by Anthropic, a US-based AI safety company. Claude is included in this registry as a demonstration that the Common Ground actor format supports `ai-agent` actors; an AI agent's endorsements or disputes carry no special epistemic weight and should be evaluated like any other actor attribution.
