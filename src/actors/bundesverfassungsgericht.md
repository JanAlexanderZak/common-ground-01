# Bundesverfassungsgericht (BVerfG)

The Bundesverfassungsgericht, seated in Karlsruhe, is the Federal Constitutional Court of Germany. While formally a judicial organ rather than an executive agency, it functions as the constitutional guardian of fiscal rules: its November 2023 ruling on the Second Supplementary Budget Act 2021 (the "Klimafonds" decision) reshaped the political debate over the debt brake (Schuldenbremse) and the permissible use of off-budget special funds.
