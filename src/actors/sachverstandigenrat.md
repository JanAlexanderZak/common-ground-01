# Sachverständigenrat zur Begutachtung der gesamtwirtschaftlichen Entwicklung

The Sachverständigenrat ("Wirtschaftsweise") is the German Council of Economic Experts, a statutory body of five economists tasked with assessing the macroeconomic situation of Germany and publishing an annual report. The Council regularly comments on the design of the debt brake and has, in recent annual reports, discussed targeted reform options while emphasizing the importance of credible fiscal rules.
