# Bundesregierung

The Bundesregierung is the executive cabinet of the Federal Republic of Germany, headed by the Federal Chancellor and composed of the federal ministers. It drafts the federal budget, proposes legislation, and represents Germany in EU and international fiscal-policy negotiations.
